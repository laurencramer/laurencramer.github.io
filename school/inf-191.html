senior design project

Winter/Spring 2019 - 20 weeks

about

Our group created a project for Apples and Oranges, whose mission is to "'take the starving out of artist” by empowering storytellers to envision their work through the lens of a creative entrepreneur." We were tasked with creating a website for small theatre owners to simplify the process of acquiring rights to plays and shows, and providing a portal to access show resources. The website would also give smaller shows an opportunity to be seen and performed by theatres, big and small. This website was intended to solve current problems that smaller theatres have when acquiring show rights. Currently, the process is unfair, difficult, time-comsuming, and complicated. Our solution was to create TheatreNow, a simple and streamlined system, give smaller theatres access to large shows, and reduce or eliminate any upfront cost to the theatre required to perform a show.
