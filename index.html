about me

My name is Lauren, but almost everyone I know calls me Larn. I'm from Playa del Rey, California, where I currently reside. I moved back after recently graduating from the University of California, Irvine with a B.S. in Informatics with a specialization in Human-Computer Interaction.

I enjoy front-end developing, UI design, and UX research.

Some of my hobbies include figure skating, video games, cosplaying, and hanging out with my two cats.

In the future, I hope to pursue a Master's or Ph.D. in Data Science or Human-Computer Interaction.

UCI Senior Capstone Project - 20 weeks

Project Background: For this project, our group was "sponsored" by a company called Apples and Oranges. Apples and Oranges helps "starving" artists develop and bring their shows to life and also helps smaller theatres more effectively acquire rights to larger shows that they couldn't obtain or afford otherwise.

Project Requirements: Our task was to create a website for smaller theatre managers, owners, and producers. They wanted a simple, clean, intuitive website where theatre mananagement could apply for show rights, access all show resources, promote their shows, and track revenue.

Our Solution: TheatreNow
