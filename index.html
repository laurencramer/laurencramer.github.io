UI Designer • UX Researcher • Cat Lover • Bee Devotee

My name is Lauren, but almost everyone I know calls me Larn. I'm from Playa del Rey, California, where I currently reside. I moved back after recently graduating from the University of California, Irvine with a B.S. in Informatics with a specialization in Human-Computer Interaction. I enjoy user interface design, user experience research, and front-end development.
Some of my hobbies include figure skating, video games, cosplaying, and hanging out with my two cats.
In the future, I hope to pursue a Master's or Ph.D. in Data Science or Human-Computer Interaction.
