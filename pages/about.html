About Me

Hello! I'm Lauren Cramer.

As Project Manager and UX Designer, I am dedicated to empowering clients through innovative software solutions, strategic guidance, and user-centered design. My experience spans managing end-to-end software implementations, serving as a primary liaison between clients and internal teams, and ensuring seamless transitions from prospects to loyal customers. I collaborate cross-functionally with sales, solutions architecture, product, and engineering teams to deliver tailored solutions that align with client goals while prioritizing intuitive and engaging user experiences.

Throughout my career, I have successfully led projects focused on workflow design, data science consultation, and UX design. My proactive approach to stakeholder management and client success has driven measurable results, fostering strong relationships and long-term partnerships. I take pride in simplifying complex software implementations and designing user-friendly interfaces that maximize client engagement and satisfaction. With expertise in Agile development, budget monitoring, project tracking, documentation, and user experience design, I thrive in dynamic environments where adaptability and strategic thinking are key.

Outside of work, I live in Playa del Rey, CA. I share my home with two cats and two snakes. In my free time, I enjoy 3D modeling and printing, riding motorcycles along the coast, and diving into a good book.
